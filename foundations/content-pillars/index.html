The Five Pillars

What we make, and why

Each pillar owns one of the brand pillars from discovery and turns it into postable content. Think of them as recurring "shows" on the page — not random posts, but a rhythm the audience learns to expect.

1
Trust · Consistent / Reliable

Mainit, Sariwa, Laging Pareho

Turns "Trusted" & "Quality" into a daily promise
The pillar that ends the gamble. Joy never has to wonder if the bread is hot today. We show up with the same fresh quality every single morning — and we prove it.

Fresh-bake moments"Ano'ng mainit ngayon"Behind the counterMorning ritual posts

Getting the Balance Right

The content mix

A starting weighting for the calendar — heavy on what Joy engages with and shares, with the emotional Caregiver content saved for hero moments. Tune it as the data comes in.

Diskarteng Wais (Value)30%
Daan Muna Kay Tita Julie (Warmth)25%
Mainit, Sariwa (Trust)20%
Tinapay ng Bayan (Neighborhood)15%
Alagang Nanay (Caregiver)10%

How to use this

Plan a week by pillar, not by post. Fill the high-frequency buckets (Value, Warmth) first — these are your daily bread. Drop in Trust and Neighborhood for texture. Then build the month around one or two Caregiver hero moments that carry the campaign feeling.

Directional, not fixed — review quarterly against reach & engagement.
